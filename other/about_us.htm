About Us

Meet the members of the CTF Academy Team.

Jacob Corley is pursuing a bachelor’s degree in IT with a concentration in cybersecurity at Middle Georgia State University. He is also an EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacker and Certified Threat Intelligence Analysis. Jacob has competed in several cybersecurity CTF competitions where he placed 21st individually in the nation, and as leader the MGA CyberKnights, he led a team that placed 12th. Jacob used his skills as a certified cybersecurity specialist to develop lessons and challenges for the CTF Academy training program.

James Corley is pursuing a bachelor’s degree in IT with a concentration in web applications development at Middle Georgia State University. He has had experience developing webpages and web applications for different academic projects. James recently won a Certificate of Academic Excellent for his concentration in Web Applications Development. James used his experience with Bootstrap, jQuery, and JavaScript to create CTFA’s responsive layout and interactive challenges.

John Reiman has over 30 years of security, military, and law enforcement experience. Most of this time has been spent as a Facility Security Officer supporting classified contracts for a variety of Government entities. He has also worked as an Information System Security Officer (ISSO) and Information System Security Manager (ISSM), Police Officer, Military Police Officer, and combat veteran. Mr. Reiman has degrees in Management, Criminal Justice, and in 2019 will graduate from Middle Georgia State University with a CyberSecurity degree.
